# AI Infrastructure

**Status:** Active

## Description
The buildout of compute, networking, and power infrastructure required to train and serve AI models at scale. Hyperscaler capex is projected at $660-690B in 2026, with ~75% ($450B+) directed at AI-related infrastructure.

## Key drivers
- Hyperscaler capex acceleration: MSFT $190B, GOOGL $175-185B, AMZN $200B, META $115-135B, ORCL $50B
- GPU demand from training and inference workloads
- Datacenter power and cooling requirements
- Custom silicon development (Google TPUs, Amazon Graviton/Trainium)
- Edge AI inference buildout
- AI software monetisation (Palantir Aleph platform +85% rev growth)
- Edge compute and agentic AI infrastructure ([[NET]] Workers platform)
